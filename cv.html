Computer Vision

Written in C++

In this project, I utilized seam carving to resize pictures in a way so as to avoid meaningful changes in the content. In order to decide which seams to remove, I calculated the energy of each seam, which is the change between the pixel to the left of the seam and the pixel to the right of the seam. I then found the seams with the lowest energy and removed them to make the image smaller.
